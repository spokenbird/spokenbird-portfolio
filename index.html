Hi, I'm Alex Gonzalez

A Software Engineer living in the San Francisco Bay Area.

I'm a Software Engineer with a degree in Technocultural Studies from the University of California, Davis. I'm interested in the intersection of technology with art, culture and science. I have 7 years of experience designing and building websites with Drupal and Wordpress.

Outside the technology sphere I've enjoyed a career as an electronic music producer spanning the last decade. In addition to playing at music festivals and nightclubs across the US, I've had the opportunity to work on music in spots and commercials for Adobe, Seagate and Facebook. I also enjoy camping, hiking, creative writing and video games. .
